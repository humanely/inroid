Signup Cloud | On-Premise

TALKING is available for cloud as well as on-premise deployment.

Install

Download and Install the Sensor Bots® from your deployment server. The bots are available for Windows, Linux, and Mac OSX. TALKING is PLug'n'Play , as it requires no integration or further coding for usage.

Setup

Setup your organisation on the server. Provide Name, Department Name, Team Name, and System ID for each staff.

Mapping

It is completed in few days. The key tasks self-performed by the TALKING system are to build process diagrams based on repeatability of tasks by every staff, and their collaboration, to learn all the key parameters itself.

This phase and usually lasts 48 hours for a well-planned and implemented system where all necessary processes and staff are available for business-as-usual.

Design Experiences

For monitoring quality of all customer interactions, create KPIs in dashboard and share it across your team and department.

Efficiency

  • Level 1 - Optimization

    Lean, Six Sigma, Error Proofing TALKING provides workflows, business rules, the trends and patterns of all events into your business. At times, it is more data than tradionally required. Therefore, your expert team should have dream state of zero effort on data collection and sampling. Perform process optimization by process optimisation tools like Lean, six sigma, and error proofing, with a team of experts in respective fields. This should be aligned to your organisational goals and full support from management.

  • Level 2 - Automation

    Robotics Download business rules in JSON or XML to build robots for any of the process steps. The business rules confirm to standard "Virtual Keycodes" for all interaction and can be portaed to build executables.

  • Level 3 - Enterprise IT

    Business Analysis Enterprise IT systems are complex and required granular details for creating robust enterprise scale technology solutions. This task has become easier with TALKING workflows, business rules and demand patterns. Now you can complete the story boarding, prioriization and test setup in fraction of time.

  • Level 4 - Smart Contracts

    Bloackchain The smart contracts technology can decentralise the trust and increase confidence at the same time. The business rules from TALKING system can be inherently converted to smart contracts.
